## 💡 Key Business Insights

- West Region generated the highest sales.
- Technology category recorded the highest profit.
- Consumer segment contributed the largest share of revenue.
- Sales peaked during November and December, indicating strong seasonal demand.
- Higher discount levels reduced overall profitability.

---

## 📋 Strategic Recommendations

- Improve profitability in the Central Region.
- Increase investment in Technology products.
- Optimize discount strategies.
- Strengthen customer retention programs.
- Improve inventory planning for seasonal demand.
